About me

I am a Hedrick Assistant Adjunct Professor at the University of California, Los Angeles. My mentor is Alexander Merkurjev.
Before this, I was a PhD student at the University of British Columbia, working under the supervision of Zinovy Reichstein. Here is my CV.
